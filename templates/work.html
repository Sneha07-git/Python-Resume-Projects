001 : Edunguru Inc, Vile Parle

I have worked on a Retainership basis at Edunguru Inc (Aug 18 - Mar 20) as a IT Trainee. This job intentionally taken by me because after giving tons of interviews for a Software Development profile though I never got selected for any of them! so, I decided to take this opportunity as only to gain industry experience. I got this job after a year of my graduation. So, Edunguru Inc is an e-Learning startup (https://edunguru.com/) at Vile Parle area; having partnership with Sahara India Pariwar. Working in a startup was exciting as that was my best decision ever to take this opportunity! because I got to know working of every department very keenly even though I have worked under every department from IT to Sales & Marketing. We were about 10-20 people including seniors. Our headquarter is at Lucknow. I majorly worked under IT department. My work mostly aligned with Product quality assurance. Frequently I was in contact with our development team to fix bugs & to assign a task if any new modification required. I had in communication with our clients. From there I experienced the frequently changing requirements of clients as they want quick fixes! I learned so much things over there. This startup helped me to build my Confidence, my overall Persona & how to keep yourself calm in Critical Situation!
